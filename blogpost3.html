Easy-to-make no-bake Malteser cheesecakes

07/04/2020 20:12

Have you ever read a title that rhymed so elegantly? Cream cheese and Maltesers are two top tier foodstuffs that I have combined to create (drumroll) the easiest and most hassle-free no-bake cheesecake recipe... Ever!

I am usually camping so I never have any baking equipment. I don't even have an oven most of the time. So, I have tried to make this recipe work using just the basics: no fancy equipment, hand-whisked, and everything can be bought from your local supermarket for under a tenner.

Enough of my rambling. Let's get into it...
